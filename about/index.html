Our Mission - we want to make you the team you always wanted to be.

Unplugged values the importance of you. We strive to create a place where you can relax, clear your mind and reset. Our goal is to make you and your colleagues a team in and out of the workplace.

We are located near Lake Geneva in the state of Wisconsin. Being this close to a beautiful body of water enables the mind to unwind and promotes a state of tranquility. This is a one of a kind experience that will benefit you and your team greatly in so many ways.
