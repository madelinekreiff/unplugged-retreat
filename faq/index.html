Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

We will have a shuttle pick you up at the airport. If you are from the area, a shuttle will pick you up from a designated meeting spot.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Any team! We are more than happy to accomodate any team from any industry or location. Our packages are customized to fit each team's specific needs.

What size teams can you accommodate?

We can accommodate any size team up to a maximum of 100 teammates.

Is there really no wifi available?

We have designated computer rooms with excellent wifi, should you need to use the internet at any time.
